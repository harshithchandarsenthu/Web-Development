What is Bingsu?

Bingsu is a popular Korean dessert made with finely shaved ice, sweet toppings, fruits, condensed milk, and other delicious ingredients. It is especially enjoyed during hot summer days.

Popular Bingsu Flavors

  • Mango Bingsu
  • Strawberry Bingsu
  • Green Tea Bingsu
  • Chocolate Bingsu
  • Red Bean Bingsu (Patbingsu)

Fun Fact!

The most traditional version of Bingsu is called Patbingsu, which is topped with sweet red beans.
