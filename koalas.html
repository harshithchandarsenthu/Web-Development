Koalas





Animal:

Koala


Life Span:

10-20 yrs.


Habitat:

Australian eucalyptus tree forests/ plain.


Features:

No Paw Pads, Two Thumbs, Grey and Thick Fur, Curved Spine, Fused Toes, etc!


Fun Fact!:

Koalas have slow metobalism periods which last for 20 hours, meaning that they can go on for hours without eating!
