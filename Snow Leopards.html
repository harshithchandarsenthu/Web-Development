The Snow Leopard

Snow Leopards

About:
The Snow leopard is a rare wild cat found in the mountains of Asia.

Habitat:
It lives in cold, rocky mountains like the Himalayas.

Diet:
Snow leopards eat wild goats, sheep, hares, and small animals.

Appearance:
They have thick spotted fur, wide paws, and a long fluffy tail.

Fun Fact:
Snow leopards are called the "Ghost of the Mountains" because they are very hard to find.

Conservation:
They are a Vulnerable species and are protected by conservation efforts.
