Benefits of diets:

Following a diet is crucial for those who need to gain or lose weight, but never too much of weight loss/gain.

One's diet must be balanced because:

  1. Provides appropriate and needed nutrition.
  2. Makes your body fit.
  3. Improves immunity and controls your intake of junk food.

What can be done to balance your diet?

  • Measure your portions of protein, fat and carbohydrate intake, but still have them in a sufficient amount.
  • Have more fruits and vegetables to satisfy the amount of vitamins and minerals your body needs.

Other things that a diet can provide:

Long-lasting energy
Provides more energy from healthier food and more metabolism takes place.
Healthier internal and external body health
Human features like ut health and enamel strenght can be improved.
